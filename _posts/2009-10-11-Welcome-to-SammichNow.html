Welcome to SammichNow.com.  You're probably wondering what the hell this site is all about.  It's about Sammiches and it isn't.  The sammich is an amazing piece of culinary delight.  Statistics (google) show that the average person eats more sammiches now than ever before in the history of consumption.  Why just today, I had a sammich.  If that isn't proof that the sammich is the height of edible fashion, then I suggest you go back to school.  Many people think that the sammich is just some simple pieces of bread with some random stuff in the middle.   While a unsophisticated person (red neck) might be willing to accept such an uncomplicated definition, as true connoisseurs of the sammich, we know that they are so much more.  Be you a layman or a fellow connoisseur, we welcome you to join us as we explore all the Sammiches and Sammich fixings the world has to offer.

Now as I said before, this isn't JUST about the amazing Sammich.  We're humans too after all.  In addition to hearing all about where you can find the best Sammiches or how to make your own, we're also going to share with you some stories from our lives. As a group of 20-30-40 something guys, we do some ridiculous and pretty dumb shit.  So if you're not in it for the food, come for the fun.
