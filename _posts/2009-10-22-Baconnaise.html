For those of you that are not enamored by all things bacon related, go to hell.  For the rest of you, I’d like to tell you about the best thing to put on a piece of bread since bacon: Baconnaise.  Baconnaise is a beautiful fusion (they had sex) between bacon and mayonnaise.  Everyone says that everything tastes better with bacon and this is an easy way to make sure every one of your sammiches doesn’t have to suffer from being baconless.  Now I’m sure you’re thinking, “Bacon mayo?  Why don’t you just stick an IV of Crisco right into my fucking heart?”.  Well, truth be told, Baconnaise isn’t that unhealthy.  First off, it’s  not only vegetarian, but it’s also kosher!  So all you Hebrews and Shebrews out there, don’t let your fear of the pork and of being porked stop you from smearing Baconnaise all over each other and just going crazy.  And now, for those of you that love your nutritional values, here are some stats for you:

The first question you as a non-idiot should ask is, “What is this regular mayo?”  Well if you’d believe what’s written on the internet, then the “regular mayo” is based on an average of 3 leading mayonnaise brands.  So, with that in mind, what do we know?  Well, first off, we already know that bacon tastes FANTASTIC!  But that’s not exactly from the stats.  First, Baconnaise  is no worse for you than regular mayo, in fact in some categories it’s BETTER!  Second, there is a LITE brand that is even more healthy for you. Now, we could also put up the stats for what it’s like putting this on a sammich vs. actual bacon (in any possible situation real bacon should be used…if possible, use both bacon and Baconnaise) but you can just go here to see it.  So do yourself a favor and find a store that sells it or buy some online.  Up soon we’ll review another favorite of ours, BaconSalt.  For now, get yourself some Baconnaise-or possibly better, get some bacon lip balm and lather your lips up.
