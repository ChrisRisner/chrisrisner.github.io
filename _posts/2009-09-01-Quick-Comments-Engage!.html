In an effort to make it easier for my 3 loyal readers to comment on the mind blowingly  interesting stuff I write about, I’ve introduced (re: stolen) quick quoting from the main page.  How does this work you ask?  Easier than pie.  If you happen upon the main page and find an article that seems to catch your fancy, all you need to do is hover over the “Comments” link and click it.  After some fancy animation (which still needs to be fixed) you’ll be shown the comments right on that same page.  WOW!!!!  After that you are free to comment away.
