I awoke today to the beauty that only a snowy road that is never plowed could provide.  After getting ready for work, I realized that I was lucky enough to have run low on gas driving home from Detroit the night before so I’d be stopping at the gas station.  After stopping for gas I went to turn the Jetta back on and it just wouldn’t turn over.  Sure as shit, the car is sick.  Now, I know I’m months behind getting a few issues fixed, but they didn’t seem to be causing any issues.  Suffice to say, I quickly called up AtomicInternet for auto assistance.  In a flash he was over to jump my Jetta back into action.  The even wouldn’t have been complete without a fantastic picture to commemorate.
